About - Roni Küppers

Roni Küppers

Hi!

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Methodology at LSE.
You can check my institutional page here.

My work is structured around an overarching fascination with the links between collective identity and political participation, the way they feed into each other, and how these links explain our capacity to imagine/enact political change. The broader objective is to understand and address the challenge of materialising the fundamental human capacity to bond, coalesce, and collaborate, in a context of increasing social complexity and mounting global challenges.

At an empirical level, this work has taken shape in various ways: for instance, my PhD research is concerned with understanding how populism is grounded in collective identities, and what implications these identities have more broadly for political change.
